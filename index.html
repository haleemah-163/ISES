Catch a glimpse

The Board of Directors of the International Solar Energy Society (ISES) meet every year to discuss strategies, new directions and report on the progress made by the Society. Catch a glimpse of the ISES Board of Directors and Headquarters staff at the 2017 ISES Board Meeting in our videos and photo slideshows on the ISES YouTube channel.

ISES informs and connects its extensive, diverse membership of researchers, academics, professionals, practitioners, businesses, decision makers, and advocates in more than 100 countries.
